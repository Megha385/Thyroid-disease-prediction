üìö Learn About Thyroid

ü¶ã What is the Thyroid?

Your thyroid is a small, butterfly-shaped gland in your neck. Think of it as your body's engine controller - it helps control how fast or slow your body works!

‚ö° What Does It Do?

The thyroid makes hormones that control:

  • How fast your heart beats
  • How quickly you burn calories
  • Your body temperature
  • Your energy levels

üö® Common Problems

Hyperthyroid: When it works too fast (like a racing car engine)

Hypothyroid: When it works too slow (like a sluggish engine)

ü©∫ Signs to Watch For

  • Feeling very tired or very energetic
  • Weight changes without trying
  • Feeling too hot or too cold
  • Heart beating fast or slow
